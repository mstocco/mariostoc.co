### WORKOUT 1 &mdash; OPEN WATER SWIM
Swim 45 minutes: 
3x 3 minutes easy swimming to warm up. R 1:00 after each. 
3x 8 minutes, nice steady effort. Find your groove. Swim straight. Keep a steady rhythm. R 2:00 after each. 
3 minutes easy swimming to cool down. 

### WORKOUT 2 &mdash; BIKE
Easy spin to the Lake.  Easy spin home from the lake.


### NOTES
Before the day got ahead of me, I did yesterday's run before work.  This may have been the best run I've had here at home and while wearing the _Takumi Sen 8_ shoes.  I laced them a bit different this morning.

Cuboid joint was a lot less sore and I added a couple short strides to finish the last 9 minute rep.  Runner's high.

A bit mentally tired through mid-day but once at the lake, I was fully present for the workout.  I swam the warm-up long to get myself to the other end of the lake.  That set me up for 3 lengths of 8 minutes of straight-line swimming.  I seemed to go a touch further swimming looking into the sun; weird.

Ride to/from the lake un-eventful.
