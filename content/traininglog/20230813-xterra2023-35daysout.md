### NOTES
The ride on _Interurban/Goward_ to the dirt on _Meadowbrook_ was 30 minutes, roughly the same time on asphalt as _Highland Road_ up to the north end of _Thetis Lake Park._

_Executive West_ heading up hill had me blown up; too heavy/lack of fitness.

Did ok on the blue trails: _Shock Treatment_, _Fire Drill_, _Ruffles_.


The black trail, _Organ Doner_ had me walking and turned around.  _Musty Knuckle_ was an unmarked blue trail that took me back to a fire road.

Back on blue with _Crossover_ becoming _Botton Feeder_ for a climb up.  Had a minor fall on what should have been an easy-ish switchback.  I realized at that point I had not hit the sugar bottle yet.  Problem solved.

More blue trails for the trip downslope, _Fun Trail_ (as the name suggests), staying in the park on _Say Ah!_.  Ended up on a trail that my head unit said was _Madonna_ (it wasn't) and I walked some challenging sections.

_One Less Car_ to the lower bit of _Shock Treatment_ was cleaned too.

I explored an unsanctioned trail off _Executive West_ and crashed.  Bruised left bicep and left calf.  I picked myself up, pedalled back to the proper trail and headed home.

Good stimulus in the legs, got to pay attention to effort levels on the blue trails and found a fitness limit too.

Still would like to improve.
