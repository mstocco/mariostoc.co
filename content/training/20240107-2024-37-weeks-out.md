### WEEKLY GOAL
Back to work this week; keep the train on the tracks.

### SUMMARY
Total Training Time: **7&frac34; hours**

I feel like I was **STILL RECOVERING**

Two focused sessions in the pool this week and thankful to have an engaging indoor riding setup; the weather turned really cold the back half of this week.

There is hope and there is reality.
