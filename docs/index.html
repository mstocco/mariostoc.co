SOCIALLY PROFILED

In January 2017, I commited to running a popular local 10KM race. Barely able to jog around the block, I lasted just 2 days before I found myself injured. I was running again by March and at the end of April, I crossed that finish line.

Following that experience, I signed up for the Goodlife Fitness Victoria Marathon; I had the run of my life.

Now 3 more marathons later, this site mostly serves as my training journal as I prepare to complete my first Ironman distance triathlon.

I hope you enjoy the read.
