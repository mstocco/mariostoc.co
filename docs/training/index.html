TRAINING LOG

There are many internet services for tracking physical exercise; I have accounts with a few of those platforms. They are effective at organizing datapoints collected from an electronic device.

The inconvience of a week-long outage on one of those platform prompted me to build this training journal.

What I created was a journal focused on the daily/weekly experience of training and less about the numbers.

HOW IT WORKS (TLDR)

The two most important weeks are always my CURRENT and PREVIOUS weeks of training.

A CALENDAR gives an overview of time invested/remaining until the last race of the year.

Simple text files, one per week, are used to jot down daily activities. The text is written in a shorthand format called "markdown".

An application reads the text files, producing this training journal (and entire website).

The application I created for myself is called a Static Site Generator. If you are interested, the computer code for all of this is hosted and downloadable via GitHub

RELEVANCY

  • Those embarked on their own similar journey and want to read about my process.
  • My boss who may be wondering "the real reason" we are getting too close to our project's deadline.
